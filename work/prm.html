Pacific Residential Mortgage (PRM) is a local mortgage company that started in 2004. Unlike many other mortgage companies, PRM has their own bank in-house which allow for in-house funding/processing and cuts down on some on some of the wait time. Their goal is to streamline the mortgage process, and provide local and outstanding customer service. I worked at PRM as a Graphic Designer and Website Manager.
