Northwest Indian Language Institute

Collateral


Illustration - Children's Story Book


The NILI is non-profit funded through the University of Oregon. Their goal is to help keep Native American Languages thriving by offering language-teaching tools to local tribes, and teaching Native American language classes.

I started out illustrating children’s books that were written in Chinukwawa, and later moved on to designing marketing collateral and ads for the NILI’s summer program.
